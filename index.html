About me

I am an economist specializing in macroeconomic monitoring and policy analysis, currently based in Washington, D.C. My work sits at the intersection of rigorous quantitative analysis and real-world policy impact.

I have contributed to high-stakes analytical work at the IMF, World Bank, and Inter-American Development Bank, focusing on fiscal sustainability, current account dynamics, and growth analytics in Latin America, the Caribbean, and globally.

I hold an MSc in Economics from the London School of Economics and a Bachelor's degree with honours from Pontifical Javeriana University in Bogotá, Colombia.
